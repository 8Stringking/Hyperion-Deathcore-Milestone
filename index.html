Hyperion is a online music project based in Devon England. After playing as a live band for many years we are now solely focused on producing the most destructive albums possible. What you will hear from Hyperion is the product of nearly 12 years writing Deathcore and will hear some of the heaviest breakdowns ever, blended with absolute speed and precision.

The Fall Of The City States

We are excited to announce that in early 2022 we will be releasing the follow up to The Nibiru Cataclysm. This new record The Fall Of The City States is a much different record. It takes more influences from bands such as Thy Art Is Murder, Shadow Of Intent, Fit For An Autopsy and stears away from the orchestral style of The Nibiru Cataclysm to deliver something that we believe to be heavier. It is about the Fall of the city states in ancient Greece, when the persians from the Achaemenid Empire stormed the Aegean, and the stories and conflicts of war that raged.
